Caffeine Curiousity

An evaluation of the Caffeine Database

Objectives Analysis

How much caffeine is too much?

The primary objective of this assignment was to evaluate the caffeine database and investigate the amount of caffeine in energy drinks and various foods. Data visualizations, classifications and k-means clustering were utilized in this project.

  • Python
