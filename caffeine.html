Caffeine Curiousity

An evaluation of the Caffeine Database

Objectives Analysis

How much caffeine is too much?

The primary objective of this assignment was to evaluate the caffeine database and investigate the amount of caffeine in energy drinks and various foods. What classifies something as having extreme caffeine? According to this source how can we cluster caffeine content, predict categories and give an overall rating for drinks and foods.
