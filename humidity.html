Humidity

There is not much correlation between latitude and humidity, there seems to be a mixed bag of results. Locations further from the equator had 0 humidity while a few others had close to 80% humidity. Some locations almost very close to the equator had 20% humidity as well, further analysis would need to be done to draw sounds conclusions.
